## How it works

1. **Connect a source** — Deploy from GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket, Gitea, Docker
   registries, or a direct ZIP upload. No lock-in to any single provider.
2. **AI prepares the build** — The deployment assistant scans your repository,
   detects the stack, and generates a production-ready Dockerfile, Docker
   Compose configuration, and environment variable scaffolding. Every artifact
   is an editable draft you approve before deploy.
3. **Deploy to managed hosting** — Ship to fully managed servers in minutes
   with automatic SSL, zero-downtime rolling updates, and PR preview
   environments.
4. **Operate with AI** — Real-time logs and metrics feed AI anomaly detection.
   Errors are diagnosed, tickets are filed automatically, and self-healing
   actions run with human approval for high-impact changes.
